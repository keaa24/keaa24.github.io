Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 19 March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application "Nöbetçi Eczane" accesses, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the app.

Nöbetçi Eczane is an informational application that displays publicly available on-duty pharmacy information for selected cities. The app does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or health monitoring services.

1. Information We Access or Collect

The app may access or process the following categories of data:

  • Selected city information (for example, Istanbul, Ankara, or Izmir) in order to show the relevant on-duty pharmacies.
  • Device and technical information, such as device model, operating system version, app version, language, crash data, and basic diagnostic information.
  • IP address and network request metadata that may be processed by API providers, hosting providers, Google Firebase infrastructure, or ad services when requests are made.
  • Advertising identifiers and ad interaction data when advertisements are shown through Google AdMob.
  • Usage data, such as general interaction with screens and features, for app functionality, diagnostics, and service improvement.

2. Health Data Disclosure

Although this application provides information about on-duty pharmacies, it does not collect, store, share, or process any personal health data.

Specifically, the app does not collect or process:

  • Medical records
  • Prescription information
  • Diagnosis information
  • Treatment history
  • Symptoms
  • Insurance information
  • Test results
  • Health conditions or diseases
  • Any other user-provided medical or health-related personal information

The app does not request access to health records from any device, third-party service, or external platform.

The app does not create, maintain, infer, or store a health profile about the user.

The only user input relevant to app functionality is the city selected by the user. This city selection is used solely to display publicly available pharmacy information and is not associated with any health condition, diagnosis, treatment, or personal medical need.

The pharmacy-related information displayed in the app, such as pharmacy name, address, phone number, district, and map location, is publicly available business information and is not personal health data.

Therefore, the app does not process Health Data as defined under Google Play policies.

3. How We Use Information

Information may be used for the following purposes:

  • To show on-duty pharmacies for the selected city
  • To retrieve and display pharmacy address, phone, and map information
  • To improve app stability, performance, and user experience
  • To cache pharmacy data temporarily for faster loading and reduced repeated requests
  • To display advertisements through supported advertising services
  • To detect errors, misuse, abuse, or technical issues

4. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services that process certain data as part of their infrastructure:

  • Google AdMob
    Advertising ID, device information, ad interaction data, and ad performance data may be processed for ad delivery and measurement.
  • Firebase Realtime Database
    Public pharmacy listing results may be temporarily cached to improve speed and reduce repeated requests. Technical request data may be processed by Google infrastructure.
  • External Pharmacy Data API Providers
    The selected city parameter may be sent to retrieve the relevant pharmacy list. Network metadata such as IP address or technical request headers may be processed by the provider.

5. Data Storage and Retention

Pharmacy listing data may be cached temporarily in Firebase Realtime Database to improve app speed and reduce repeated API calls.

Cached pharmacy data is generally retained for a limited period, typically less than 24 hours, depending on app logic.

The app is not designed to permanently store personal user profiles or personal health records.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data.

Data may be shared only as necessary with service providers that support app functionality, such as advertising, hosting, database, analytics, and API infrastructure providers.

7. Data Deletion

The app does not maintain a user account system and does not store personal user profiles. If you have a privacy-related request, you may contact us at the email address below.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information processed through the app. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

9. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children for the purpose of knowingly collecting personal data. If you believe data has been provided inappropriately, please contact us.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
